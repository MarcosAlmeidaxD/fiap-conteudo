# Cap 04 - Self-Service BI RevFinal.pdf

**File type:** PDF | **Processed:** 2026-04-22

## TL;DR
A masterclass on shifting from IT-dependent reporting to user-driven "Self-Service" BI, covering everything from data schemas to NPS case studies.

## What's Inside
* **The 5-Level Analytics Ladder:** Breaks down Descriptive (past), Diagnostic (why), Predictive (future), Prescriptive (actions), and Cognitive (AI) methods.
* **Data Architecture for Humans:** Compares Star Schema, Snow-flake, and Flattened Tables to explain which models actually work for non-technical users.
* **Tool Scouting:** A high-level comparison of market leaders like Power BI, Tableau, Qlik, and MicroStrategy.
* **The 4-Step SSBI Journey:** A roadmap from raw data connection and prep to building custom views and sharing dashboards.
* **NPS Practical Application:** A specific deep dive into calculating and visualizing Net Promoter Scores using real-world logic.

## Worth Knowing
This guide emphasizes that successful Self-Service isn't just about giving people tools—it's about IT transitioning from "report builders" to "data governors" who ensure the foundation is clean enough for others to build on.
