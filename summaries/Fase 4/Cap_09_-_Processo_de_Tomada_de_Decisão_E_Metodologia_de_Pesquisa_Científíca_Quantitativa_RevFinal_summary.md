# Cap 09 - Processo de Tomada de Decisão E Metodologia de Pesquisa Científíca Quantitativa RevFinal.pdf

**File type:** PDF | **Processed:** 2026-04-22

## TL;DR
A roadmap for turning raw data into business decisions using the three pillars of Data Science and established frameworks like KDD and CRISP-DM.

## What's Inside
- **The Trinity:** Detailed breakdown of IT (Python/Cloud), Math/Stats (Machine Learning/Algebra), and Business Domain expertise.
- **KDD Process:** The 5-step flow for Knowledge Discovery in Databases (Selection, Pre-processing, Transformation, Data Mining, and Interpretation).
- **CRISP-DM Framework:** The 6-phase lifecycle covering Business Understanding, Data Prep, Modeling, and Deployment.
- **KDD vs. CRISP-DM:** A direct comparison (Quadro 1) showing how these two methodologies map to one another.
- **Quantitative Research:** Classifications for quantitative data and a matrix of research methods vs. data collection techniques.

## Worth Knowing
Data Science is framed here as a "science" first; it’s useless unless it's answering a specific business question. Also, the "Unicorn" data scientist is treated as a myth—real-world success relies on multidisciplinary teams rather than a single person knowing everything.
