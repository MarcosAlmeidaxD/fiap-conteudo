# Cap 03 - Arquitetura de business intelligence RevFinal.pdf

**File type:** PDF | **Processed:** 2026-04-22

## TL;DR
A comprehensive blueprint for building Business Intelligence systems, mapping the journey from raw data to actionable executive dashboards.

## What's Inside
* **The 8-Layer Architecture:** A stack covering everything from initial data sources and ETL to storage (DW), security, and maintenance.
* **ETL Breakdown:** Specific phases for extracting, cleaning (Transforming), and loading data, including the trade-offs between full and incremental loads.
* **Modeling Universes:** A direct comparison between Relational (transactional) and Dimensional (analytical) modeling, featuring Star and Snowflake schemas.
* **Data Varieties:** Practical definitions of structured vs. unstructured data, including how to handle modern sources like IoT devices and social media feeds.
* **Operational Health:** Guidelines for monitoring system performance and ensuring data governance/security compliance.

## Worth Knowing
The guide emphasizes that modern BI is moving away from purely internal data; it specifically flags integrating social media trends (Instagram/TikTok) with POS systems to predict demand and optimize inventory in real-time. It also notes that the "Fact Table" is the literal heart of analytical modeling—if that's messy, your insights will be too.
