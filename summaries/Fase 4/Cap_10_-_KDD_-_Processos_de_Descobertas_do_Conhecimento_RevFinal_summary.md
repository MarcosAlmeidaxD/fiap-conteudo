**File type:** PDF | **Processed:** 2026-04-22

## TL;DR
A comprehensive breakdown of the KDD (Knowledge Discovery in Databases) pipeline, mapping the journey from messy raw data to actionable patterns.

## What's Inside
* **The 5-Step KDD Workflow:** Breaks down the sequence of Selection → Preprocessing → Transformation → Data Mining → Interpretation.
* **Data Prep Toolkit:** Specific techniques for PCA (dimensionality reduction), binning, dummy variables, and Max-Min normalization.
* **Architectural Shifts:** Compares traditional ETL/Data Warehousing against modern ELT, Data Lakes, and Delta Lakes.
* **The "Big Three" Distinction:** Definitions to stop you from mixing up AI (mimicking humans), Machine Learning (predictive algorithms), and KDD (the knowledge-seeking process).
* **CRISP-DM Framework:** An introduction to the industry-standard methodology for structuring data projects.

## Worth Knowing
Data Mining is often used as a buzzword for the whole process, but here it's strictly defined as just one step—the heavy lifting actually happens during the preprocessing and transformation stages. Most of the technical failures in KDD occur because these early steps weren't "oriented" toward a specific business goal.
