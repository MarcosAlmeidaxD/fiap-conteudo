## TL;DR
A tactical guide on moving from dry data dumps to narrative-driven insights by tailoring the message to specific personas and delivery formats.

## What's Inside
- **Narrative Arc:** Maps the "Little Red Riding Hood" story to business data to show how to structure tension, climax, and resolution.
- **Audience Segmentation:** Frameworks for gauging credibility and prior knowledge using personas.
- **Live vs. Static Formats:** Tactical differences between high-pressure presentations and "non-live" written reports.
- **Visual Resources:** Guidelines for choosing between dashboards, combined Excel charts, and formal financial reports.

## Worth Knowing
The text argues that storytelling isn't just for engagement—it’s a specific defense mechanism against "closed-minded" executives who default to rejecting data that contradicts their intuition. Use the narrative structure to bypass organizational resistance before the Q&A even starts.
