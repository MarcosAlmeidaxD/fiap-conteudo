# Cap 06 - Data Storytelling - Dados e narrativas RevFinal.pdf

**File type:** PDF | **Processed:** 2026-04-22

## TL;DR
A strategic guide to moving beyond raw numbers by blending data, visuals, and narrative to drive business decisions.

## What's Inside
- **The Core Trinity**: Explaining how Data (to explain), Narrative (to engage), and Visuals (to enlighten) intersect to create a "Data Story."
- **Contextual Framing**: Specific techniques for defining your audience and objective before you even touch a chart.
- **The "Margarine" Effect**: Historical examples showing how narratives shift from "comfort" to "health" to "flavor" based on changing data and social contexts.
- **Communication Mechanisms**: A breakdown of the continuous sequence between message, channel, and receiver.
- **Evolutionary Roots**: Why humans are hardwired for stories, from 44,000-year-old cave paintings to modern BI dashboards.

## Worth Knowing
The guide argues that storytelling is actually a cognitive survival tool—originally used to protect us from predators around a campfire, it now protects projects from being ignored by turning "disordered thoughts" into a common conclusion.
