# 2TSCOR Challenge MaterDei 2oSem 2025 v2 RevFinal.pdf

**File type:** PDF | **Processed:** 2026-04-22

## TL;DR
Official roadmap for the 2nd semester 2025 Data Science challenge to solve patient "no-shows" and appointment conversion for the MaterDei hospital network.

## What's Inside
- **The Mission:** Use MaterDei’s anonymized data to build predictive models that increase the conversion rate of scheduled exams and consultations.
- **Team Rules:** Maximum 5 people per group, with a hard deadline for member swaps ending after Sprint 3.
- **4-Sprint Structure:** A breakdown of mandatory reports and pitches, plus "bonus" points for interactive dashboards (Power BI/Python) and real-time models.
- **Submission Protocol:** No external links allowed; everything must be uploaded directly to the FIAP portal (except for YouTube pitch links in a .txt file).

## Worth Knowing
The judges are specifically looking for "conversion by patient profile," so segmenting your data by canal and service type is non-negotiable. If you make the top three, you’re headed to the NEXT 2026 stage for cash vouchers and medals.
